# Asset Privacy And Copyright Checklist

## Repository And Video Privacy

- [ ] No credentials, API keys, private keys, tokens, authorization values, or passwords.
- [ ] No private local paths, usernames, machine names, browser profiles, or backup locations.
- [ ] No Zotero item metadata, item keys, annotations, highlights, attachment paths, or processing logs from a real library.
- [ ] No PDF pages, copyrighted article text, Obsidian vault notes, unpublished research, or purchased data.
- [ ] No personal email, notification, calendar event, message, account avatar, or unrelated browser tab.
- [ ] No Codex Session ID, private transcript, Devpost receipt, or private confirmation screenshot.
- [ ] Synthetic examples are clearly fictional or redacted and use placeholders such as `<note_root>`.

## Copyright And Brand Use

- [ ] No copyrighted music, unlicensed stock image, article screenshot, journal figure, or third-party video clip.

- [ ] Zotero, Codex, Obsidian, GitHub, YouTube, and Devpost names are used descriptively without implying endorsement.
- [ ] No third-party logo is used as project branding.

## Attribution And Licensing

## Publication Check
